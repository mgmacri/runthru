## Description
RunThru helps you get through what you need to read.

Not a speed-reading app. A completion app. Built for ADHD readers, professionals drowning in documents, and anyone who keeps opening an article only to drift away three paragraphs in.

HOW IT WORKS

RunThru presents text one word at a time using Optimal Recognition Point (ORP) pacing. Your eyes stay fixed. Your attention stays forward. The app adapts timing per word — longer words and complex punctuation get more time automatically.

You set the pace. RunThru keeps it steady.

PACED READING MODES
• Word-at-a-time with ORP anchoring — the core experience
• Sentence mode with word highlighting and auto-scroll
• Paragraph mode with timed advance
• 3D viewport with parallax depth for immersive focus sessions

ADAPTIVE FEATURES (ALL FREE, ALWAYS)
• Per-word adaptive pacing — timing adjusts to word complexity
• Adjustable letter and word spacing
• OpenDyslexic font support
• Reading ruler overlay
• Colour vision deficiency-safe themes
• Font size controls
• Reduced motion mode

BRING YOUR OWN CONTENT
• Paste from clipboard — articles, emails, notes
• Import PDFs (beta) — text extraction with page-level navigation
• Import EPUB files (beta) — chapter-level reading
• Share from any app via the system share sheet
• Instapaper integration — read your saved articles with pacing

READING ANALYTICS (ON-DEVICE ONLY)
• Session tracking — words read, duration, WPM
• 30-day WPM trend chart
• Reading streak counter
• All data stays on your device. Nothing uploaded. Ever.

DESIGNED FOR FOCUS
• Distraction-free reading viewport
• Context reveal — swipe up to see the surrounding sentence
• Gesture-driven controls — no menus to break your flow
• WPM dial for quick pace adjustment
• Warm-up ramp eases you into each session

PRIVACY BY DEFAULT
RunThru stores all reading content on your device. No accounts required. No cloud sync. No tracking. Your reading is yours.

PDF and EPUB import are functional but labelled beta — formatting edge cases exist. Plain text and clipboard work reliably across all content.

Your attention is not the problem. It just needs a better direction.
