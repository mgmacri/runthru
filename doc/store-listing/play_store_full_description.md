Paced reading that helps you finish what you start. Built for ADHD readers — works great for everyone.

RunThru presents text one word at a time with adaptive pacing. Your eyes stay fixed. Your attention stays forward. No speed-reading gimmicks — just steady, completable reading sessions.

**How it works**: Optimal Recognition Point (ORP) pacing holds each word at its natural anchor point. Timing adapts per word — longer and more complex words get more display time automatically. You set the pace. RunThru keeps it consistent.

**Paced reading modes**
• Word-at-a-time with ORP anchoring
• Sentence mode with word highlighting and auto-scroll
• Paragraph mode with timed advance
• 3D parallax viewport for immersive focus

**Bring your own content**
• Paste from clipboard — articles, emails, notes
• Import PDFs (beta) — text extraction with page navigation
• Import EPUB files (beta) — chapter-level reading
• Share from any app via the share sheet
• Instapaper integration — read your saved articles with pacing

**Accessibility features (free, always)**
• Per-word adaptive pacing
• Adjustable letter and word spacing
• OpenDyslexic font support
• Reading ruler overlay
• Colour vision deficiency-safe themes
• Font size controls
• Reduced motion support

**Reading analytics (on-device only)**
• Session tracking — words read, duration, WPM
• 30-day trend chart
• Reading streak counter
• All data stays on your device. Nothing is uploaded.

**Designed for focus**
• Distraction-free viewport
• Context reveal — swipe to see surrounding sentence
• Gesture-driven controls — no menus breaking flow
• WPM dial for quick pace changes
• Warm-up ramp for each session

**Privacy by default**
No accounts. No cloud sync. No tracking. All reading content stays on your device.

PDF and EPUB import are functional and labelled beta — some formatting edge cases remain. Clipboard and plain text work reliably for all content types.
