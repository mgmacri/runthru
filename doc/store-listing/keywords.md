### Apple Keywords Field (separate from description)
`adhd,focus,reader,rsvp,dyslexia,accessible,epub,pdf,pacing,concentration,reading tool`

Note: Apple's algorithm uses title + subtitle + keyword field for ranking.
Words already in the title ("RunThru", "Paced", "Reading") and subtitle
("Finish", "start") are excluded from the keyword field to avoid redundancy
penalties. Each keyword in the field is unique and targets a distinct query
cluster.
